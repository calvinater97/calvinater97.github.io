    Web Site's Purpose

    The purpose of this website is to provide its audience with the most accurate weather report available. The site will be very easy to navigate making it a reliable location for your all your weather needs among all ages. We will offer current weather patterns including: the temperature, weather environment, wind speed, and future weather predictions. We have all been in situations when we are planning outdoor activies but then out of nowhere the weather takes a turn for the worst. This runs your activty making it harder for you to make a new plan. With our help we plan to get rid of that uncertainty so that your activies can go as planned. It is our goal that with the help of our weather forcasting you will be able to plan outdoor activies accordingly with confidence that all will go as planned.
