    • Audience

    The target audience for this website are people that are looking to have a good time outside but want to make sure the weather will be good for them. If people want to through a party or have a BBQ our site will be there to give them current weather conditions and expected conditions.

    Scenarios

    With the help of this website you will have these scenarios answered among others:

    1. What will the temperature look like this week?
    2. Will I be able to throw my outdoor party next week?
    3. Will it be safe to travel?
    4. Can I have a BBQ without worrying about bad weather?
    5. Will the weather be good if we go to the amusement park?
    6. When is the best day to go boating?

    Personas

    • Ricardo Wolfe: Ricardo is a high school student who loves to get with his friends and have a good time together. He enjoys being outdoors and hates when the weather doesn't let him do the thinks he loves. He likes to go hiking, camping, boating, and climbing. This weekend he is planning a boating trip with his friends but wants to make sure the weather will be good so they will have a good time.
    • Nina Turnbull: Nina is newly married and has just moved into a new suburb where her and her husband don't know any of their neighbors but they would like to get to know everyone. They start planning a little party and BBQ which is something Nina loved to do where she use to live. Now that she is somewhere new she wants to make a good impression on everyone but needs the weather to hold up good for her. She will need a reliable way to know if the weather will be good for her party.
